How CultureTest works

CultureTest uses AI to generate bespoke scenario-based assessments tailored to your company's specific culture. Unlike generic personality tests, we test how candidates would actually behave in real situations at your company. The result is better hires, fewer mis-hires, and a team that fits.

Frequently asked questions

Most assessment tools use generic personality matching that candidates can easily prepare for. CultureTest creates bespoke scenario-based tests tailored to your company's specific culture and values. Every test is unique to your organisation, so candidates are evaluated on how they would actually behave in your environment.

The scenarios are unique to each company and test behavioural responses, not right or wrong answers. Because the assessments are generated specifically for your culture, there is no way to prepare a "correct" response. We are testing for genuine alignment, not rehearsed answers.
